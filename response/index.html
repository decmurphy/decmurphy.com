  • In Flight Course Corrections
In Flight Course Corrections can apply to any stage while it is still airborne. You can adjust the stage's attitude by correcting it's pitch or yaw, or even just specifying 'Gravity Turn' or 'Reverse Gravity Turn' (a good one for the re-entry/landing burns). You can also adjust a stage's throttle. This can be used to perform a 2nd stage restart.
